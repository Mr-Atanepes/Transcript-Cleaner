# Privacy Policy for Transcript Cleaner

**Last Updated:** July 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user data.

## 1. No Data Collection
The Transcript Cleaner application is a 100% client-side utility. 
* We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.
* We do NOT track your usage, device identifiers, or location.
* We do NOT require any user accounts, registrations, or sign-ups.

## 2. Local Processing Only
All text files, SRT files, VTT files, or copy-pasted transcripts that you input into the application are processed entirely locally on your own device using native web APIs. No data is ever transmitted over the network or sent to external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
If you choose to use optional third-party integrations within the app (such as external links to support the developer), those services are governed by their own respective privacy policies. 

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via our official GitHub repository.
